Measurement status "8" means no measurement
Measurement status "9" means no phenomenon

For the field "Snow cover height" (PKSN) the coded values are used:
997 - less than 0.5 cm
998 - discontinuous
999 - not possible to measure
For the "Sunshine duration" field (USLN) by March 2015, the measurement should be moved by -1 hour (e.g. from 12 o'clock to 11 o'clock).
